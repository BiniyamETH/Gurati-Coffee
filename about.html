Our Story

A Specialty Coffee Company Rooted in Ethiopia

Gurati Coffee is a specialty coffee company dedicated to bringing the rich flavors of Ethiopia to coffee roasters, cafés, and distributors worldwide.

Every bean is carefully selected to deliver exceptional quality in every shipment — grown in the forests and highlands where coffee itself was born, and handled with the care that quality deserves from farm to port.
